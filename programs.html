Our Programs

At Sunshine Academy, we offer a wide range of academic and extracurricular programs designed to engage students, nurture their talents, and foster personal growth.

Primary Education

Our primary education program focuses on foundational skills in reading, writing, math, and science, preparing students for future academic success.

Secondary Education

Our secondary program helps students explore advanced subjects and prepare for higher education or careers with a diverse curriculum in arts, sciences, and humanities.

STEM Program

Our specialized STEM program encourages students to develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills through hands-on learning in science, technology, engineering, and math.

Art and Music

We offer comprehensive programs in visual arts and music, allowing students to explore creativity and self-expression through various mediums.

Sports and Athletics

Our athletics program promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship with opportunities in team sports and individual physical activities.
